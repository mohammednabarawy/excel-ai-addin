Privacy Policy for Excel AI

Last Updated: June 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Excel AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Microsoft Excel add-in.

2. Data Collection and Storage

We do not collect, store, or transmit your data to any of our own servers.

The Excel AI add-in is a client-side application. All settings, including your API keys and configuration preferences, are stored locally within your browser's local storage.

3. Third-Party Services

The add-in communicates directly with third-party Artificial Intelligence providers (e.g., Google Gemini, OpenCode, Groq, OpenRouter) based on the API keys and endpoints you configure. When you use these services, your spreadsheet data (specifically the ranges you select or query) is sent directly to those providers to generate responses.

Please review the privacy policies of the respective AI providers you choose to use:

  • Google (Gemini)
  • Your configured custom providers

If you enable the "Use public CORS proxy" feature, your API requests will be routed through a public proxy service (e.g., corsproxy.io) to bypass browser restrictions. Please be aware that routing traffic through public proxies carries inherent privacy risks.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
